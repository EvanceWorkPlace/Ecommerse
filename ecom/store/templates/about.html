About Us

Discover our mission and how we connect technology lovers with affordable electronics.

Welcome to Electronic-Components (ElemComp)

At ElemComp, we believe that technology should be accessible to everyone — not just the newest gadgets but also high-quality used and refurbished products that still have incredible value. Our platform is designed to make buying and selling electronics easier, safer, and more transparent for individuals and small businesses.

We started our journey as a small local electronics reseller, helping communities repurpose old devices while reducing electronic waste. Over time, we realized that many people were searching for a trusted place online to find affordable components, IoT devices, and reliable second-hand electronics. That’s when ElemComp evolved into a full online marketplace — where customers can explore, compare, and purchase verified products from anywhere.

Our website serves as the backbone of our business. Built with modern web technologies, it provides an intuitive shopping experience, secure transactions, and real-time product updates. Whether you’re a student, hobbyist, technician, or entrepreneur, our online store helps you find exactly what you need — from sensors, microcontrollers, and IoT modules to complete refurbished gadgets.

At ElemComp, we’re more than a store — we’re a community of tech enthusiasts passionate about sustainability, innovation, and affordability. Every product we list is carefully inspected to meet quality standards, ensuring customers get reliable electronics at the best prices.

We’re constantly expanding our catalog and adding new features to make the experience smoother. As technology advances, our mission remains the same: connect people to technology that empowers them, saves money, and protects the planet.
